Frequently asked questions

What is Grace of the Wheel?

Grace of the Wheel is a free random weapon generator for Elden Ring all-weapons runs. Spin the wheel to be assigned a random armament, retire each weapon as you clear it so it is never rolled twice, and rank your favourites on a drag-and-drop tier list.

What is an all-weapons run in Elden Ring?

An all-weapons run is a self-imposed challenge where you beat the game using every weapon at least once. This tool tracks that run: it picks your next weapon at random and keeps count of how many of the 402 armaments you have left.

How many weapons does the wheel include?

All 402 weapons from the base game and the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC, grouped into the game's 40 weapon categories — from daggers and katanas to colossal weapons, bows, glintstone staves and sacred seals.

Can I roll from specific weapon categories?

Yes. Tick any combination of categories, or use the quick filters for Melee, Ranged, Catalysts, Shields and DLC. The wheel then rolls only from the weapons you have selected.

Does it include Shadow of the Erdtree (DLC) weapons?

Yes — every DLC weapon is included, and a single DLC toggle lets you include or exclude them if you only want a base-game run.

Is my progress saved?

Your run is saved automatically in your browser, so retired weapons and tier rankings persist between visits. You can also export a backup file and import it on another device.
